Core Tube Preamplifier Including Tube Phono Stage

Rugged stainless steel chassis.
High quality UI transformer and components.
Separate MC and MM inputs. A switch on back panel select MC or MM.
A front panel rotary switch select PHONO, CD, TUNER, LINE.
Two switchable rotary volume controls (LADDER MODE 24 STEPS) one for
each channel.
Innovative hybrid input circuitry design with six fets and one ECC82 tube for each
channel ensures very low distortion and noise.
Two LUNDAHL LL1678 transformers, with amorphous core, one for each MC input.
Separate power supply for each channel with an innovative design.
Passive rc RIAA equalization.
Point to point hand soldering.

Technical Specifications

AMPLIFICATION: MM 68 db, MC 92 db
INPUT IMPEDANCE: MM 48 kohm, MC 180 ohm (can custom adjust).
CD, LINE, TUNER, 47 kohm
OUTPUT IMPEDANCE: &lt; 2 kohm.
VACUUM TUBES: 6xECC82.and 1xECC81
DIMENSIONS:39x12x41 cm (W x H x D).
NET WEIGHT: 13 kg.
